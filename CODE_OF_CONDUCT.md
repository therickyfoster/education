## 🤝 Expected Behaviors

### 💚 In All Interactions

> [!TIP]
> **We commit to embodying these practices in every interaction:**

<details>
<summary><strong>🕊️ Compassionate Communication</strong></summary>

- **Use inclusive language** that welcomes people of all backgrounds
- **Assume positive intent** while addressing harmful impact
- **Practice active listening** and genuine curiosity about others' perspectives
- **Express disagreement respectfully** and focus on ideas, not people
- **Acknowledge mistakes gracefully** and commit to learning from them

**Examples:**
- ✅ "I'm curious about a different perspective on this..."
- ✅ "Thank you for sharing that insight. I hadn't considered..."
- ✅ "I made an error in my earlier comment and want to correct it..."
- ❌ "That's obviously wrong because..."
- ❌ "Anyone with common sense would know..."

</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>🌍 Cultural Humility</strong></summary>

- **Honor indigenous wisdom** and acknowledge land relationships
- **Recognize diverse learning traditions** beyond Western academic frameworks
- **Practice cultural curiosity** rather than cultural appropriation
- **Amplify marginalized voices** and perspectives in educational discourse
- **Question assumptions** about "universal" educational practices

**Guidelines:**
- Research cultural context before sharing traditional practices
- Credit indigenous knowledge systems appropriately
- Ask permission before adapting cultural educational approaches
- Prioritize voices from communities most affected by educational inequity

</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>🔬 Intellectual Integrity</strong></summary>

- **Cite all sources** including informal conversations and community wisdom
- **Acknowledge AI assistance** transparently in all work
- **Share uncertainties** honestly rather than overstating confidence
- **Correct misinformation** promptly and publicly when discovered
- **Respect intellectual property** while advancing open knowledge

**Best Practices:**
- Use proper attribution for all quoted material
- Share methodology behind research claims
- Acknowledge limitations in data or analysis
- Provide accessible explanations of complex concepts

</details>

### 👥 In Community Spaces

<details>
<summary><strong>🎓 Educational Discussions</strong></summary>

- **Center student wellbeing** in all educational design conversations
- **Consider implementation context** when sharing resources
- **Share both successes and failures** to support authentic learning
- **Provide constructive feedback** that builds rather than tears down
- **Ask for consent** before sharing others' classroom experiences

</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>🤖 AI Ethics Dialogue</strong></summary>

- **Acknowledge AI limitations** and potential biases in all discussions
- **Prioritize human agency** in AI-human collaboration frameworks
- **Consider long-term implications** of AI integration choices
- **Include diverse perspectives** on AI's role in education
- **Model ethical AI use** in your own contributions

</details>

## 🚫 Unacceptable Behaviors

### ⚠️ Zero Tolerance Policies

> [!DANGER]
> **The following behaviors will result in immediate action:**

<table>
<tr>
<th width="50%">🚨 **Harmful to Individuals**</th>
<th width="50%">🛑 **Harmful to Community**</th>
</tr>
<tr>
<td>

- **Harassment or bullying** of any form
- **Discriminatory language** based on identity
- **Personal attacks** or ad hominem arguments
- **Threats or intimidation** explicit or implicit
- **Doxxing or privacy violations**
- **Sexual harassment** or unwelcome advances
- **Deliberate misgendering** or identity invalidation

</td>
<td>

- **Spamming or trolling** community spaces
- **Deliberate misinformation** or bad faith arguments
- **Copyright violation** or plagiarism
- **Commercial promotion** unrelated to education
- **Disrupting community processes** or governance
- **Encouraging harmful AI use** in educational settings
- **Violating child safety** protocols or guidelines

</td>
</tr>
</table>

### 🔍 Context-Specific Guidelines

<details>
<summary><strong>📚 Educational Content Standards</strong></summary>

**Prohibited:**
- Content that promotes harmful stereotypes
- Educational approaches that cause psychological harm
- AI integration without proper ethical safeguards
- Resources that violate student privacy
- Materials that exclude or marginalize learners

**Required:**
- Age-appropriate content with clear labeling
- Inclusive representation in examples and case studies
- Ethical AI use guidelines for all activities
- Accessibility considerations in all materials
- Cultural sensitivity in global educational contexts

</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>🔬 Research & Data Standards</strong></summary>

**Prohibited:**
- Sharing identifiable student or educator data
- Research without proper ethical approval
- Misrepresenting data or methodology
- Making unsupported causal claims
- Ignoring contradictory evidence

**Required:**
- Transparent methodology documentation
- Appropriate anonymization of all data
- Acknowledgment of study limitations
- Replication-friendly data and analysis sharing
- Ethical approval documentation for human subjects research

</details>

### 📝 Reporting Process

<details>
<summary><strong>1. 🚨 Immediate Safety Issues</strong></summary>

**For urgent safety concerns:**
- 📧 Email: [admin@planetaryrestorationarchive.com](mailto:admin@planetaryrestorationarchive.com)
- 📞 Phone: [(587) 723-5486](tel:+15877235486)
- 🚨 GitHub: Use "Report Content" feature for public violations

**Response Time:** Within 6 hours during business days, 24 hours on weekends

</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>2. 📋 Detailed Reporting</strong></summary>

**Please Include:**
- 📅 **When:** Date and time of incident
- 📍 **Where:** Platform, discussion thread, or document
- 👥 **Who:** People involved (usernames/handles)
- 📖 **What:** Description of the behavior
- 🎯 **Impact:** How it affected you or others
- 📎 **Evidence:** Screenshots, links, or other documentation
- 🎭 **Preference:** How you'd like us to respond

**Response Time:** Within 48 hours with initial assessment

</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>3. 🤝 Support Needs</strong></summary>

**We Can Provide:**
- 👂 Confidential listening and validation
- 🛡️ Safety planning for ongoing situations
- 🤝 Mediation between community members
- 📚 Resources for support and healing
- 🔄 Follow-up to ensure resolution

</details>

### 🔒 Privacy & Confidentiality

> [!NOTE]
> **Your privacy is sacred to us:**

- **Confidential handling:** Reports are only shared with essential response team members
- **Anonymous options:** You can report anonymously if preferred
- **Consent-based sharing:** We won't share your identity without explicit permission
- **Documentation:** We keep records only as needed for safety and learning
- **No retaliation:** We have zero tolerance for retaliation against reporters

### 📊 Response Framework

<details>
<summary><strong>🟢 Level 1: Learning Opportunity</strong></summary>

**For:** First-time minor violations, misunderstandings, or unintentional harm

**Response:**
- Private education about community standards
- Clarification of expectations
- Resources for learning and growth
- Follow-up to ensure understanding

**Duration:** Immediate resolution

</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>🟡 Level 2: Formal Warning</strong></summary>

**For:** Repeated minor violations or moderate single incidents

**Response:**
- Formal documentation of the issue
- Explicit behavior change requirements
- Temporary moderation of contributions
- Mentorship or support resources

**Duration:** 30-90 days with review

</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>🟠 Level 3: Temporary Suspension</strong></summary>

**For:** Serious violations or pattern of harmful behavior

**Response:**
- 30-90 day suspension from community spaces
- Required completion of relevant education/training
- Structured re-entry process with support
- Community service or contribution requirements

**Duration:** 30-90 days with structured re-entry

</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>🔴 Level 4: Permanent Removal</strong></summary>

**For:** Severe violations, threats to safety, or consistent harmful patterns

**Response:**
- Permanent ban from all community spaces
- Public safety notice if appropriate
- Support for affected community members
- Legal action if necessary

**Duration:** Permanent with rare appeal options

</details>

## 🌱 Restorative Justice

### 🤝 Healing-Centered Approach

> **We believe in the possibility of transformation and the power of authentic accountability.**

<details>
<summary><strong>🕊️ For Those Who Cause Harm</strong></summary>

**We Support:**
- Taking genuine responsibility for impact
- Understanding how behavior affected others
- Learning about underlying issues and patterns
- Making meaningful amends to those harmed
- Developing new skills and awareness
- Contributing positively to community healing

**Resources:**
- 📚 Educational materials on bias, trauma, and communication
- 🤝 Mediation and conflict resolution support
- 🧠 Reflection exercises and accountability partnerships
- 🌱 Opportunities for service and community contribution

</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>💚 For Those Who Experience Harm</strong></summary>

**We Provide:**
- Immediate safety and support
- Validation of your experience
- Choice and agency in response process
- Resources for healing and recovery
- Follow-up care and check-ins
- Community support and solidarity

**Support Options:**
- 👂 Trauma-informed listening and validation
- 🛡️ Safety planning and protection measures
- 🤝 Peer support connections
- 📞 Professional counseling referrals
- 🌱 Healing circles and community care

</details>
